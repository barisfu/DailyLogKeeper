DailyLogKeeper

Keep logs of your daily activities. Create, manage, and share your logs, easily! Add a job name and title to each log to easily identify them when sending. These logs can easily be shared to Google Drive or Keep to allow for private cloud backup. Great to send logs through Gmail to a supervisor to keep track of productivity
